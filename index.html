About Me

Engineering What Comes Next

I'm a freshman in Computer Engineering at the University of Michigan, driven by a passion for building systems that bridge hardware and software. My core interests lie in Embedded Systems and Robotics—designing the low-level firmware and control logic that bring autonomous machines to life.


Whether it's writing bare-metal C++ for a microcontroller or modeling digital circuits in Verilog, I'm drawn to work where every clock cycle and every byte of memory matters.

Embedded Systems

Microcontrollers, firmware development, real-time operating systems, and hardware-software co-design.

Robotics

Sensor integration, control algorithms, and autonomous navigation systems.
